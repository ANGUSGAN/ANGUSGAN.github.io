About NIGERIA Pioneerplywood

Founded in 2019, NIGERIA Pioneerplywood has grown to become the leading manufacturer of Marine Plywood and construction templates in Nigeria.

With over 500 dedicated employees, we are committed to delivering the highest quality products to the construction industry. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Mopo, Isanlu, Kogi State, produces premium Marine Plywood that meets international standards.

As a leading enterprise, we take pride in our contribution to Nigeria's infrastructure development and construction sector growth.
